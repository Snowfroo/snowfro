Choo Choo Charles

Choo Choo Charles is an awesome spider train,
but will you dare battle him in Minecraft?

Pack Info

- Version: 1.19
- Charles has 1000 HP.
- A bossbar will display his HP.
- He will follow the nearest player.
- Has speed faster than the player.
- At half health, he gets faster.
